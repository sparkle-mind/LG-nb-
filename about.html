Mission, Vision and Values

At Laul Global Ventures, our values are the cornerstone of our success. They shape our decisions, drive our actions, and define our culture. We are guided by principles of excellence, innovation, collaboration, and social responsibility. Learn more about the values that underpin everything we do.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a global education and real estate powerhouse in Canada in five years, as driven by our diversified portfolio of colleges, international student recruitment network, and commercial and residential developments powered by human expertise and technology.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals and communities by providing transformative opportunities, redefining industry standards, and creating sustainable, long-term value. We believe that investments should not only yield strong financial returns for business but also help individuals, families, and communities prosper.

Leadership Team

Sanjay Laul, CEO

Sanjay, Chief Executive Officer of Laul Global Ventures, is a 1st-generation serial entrepreneur with a vision to pioneering transformative changes across global education. He is an investor in technology, education, and real estate, as well as a thought leader in global education, international education, sustainable internationalization, and the future of work.

He founded M Square Media (MSM) in 2012, a landmark episode in his over 25 years of operational and managerial experience:

  • Building sustainable models through common-sense insight
  • Thinking up customised solutions to international student recruitment globally
  • Conceiving, developing, and bringing to market a wide range of solutions for startups
  • Strategically forming teams and lucrative public-private partnerships to accelerate growth

With presence in over 20 countries and serving 1,400+ partner institution campuses around the world, MSM has assisted more than 186,000 students worldwide and delivered sustainable recruitment and internationalisation solutions through in-country offices; the AI-powered edtech platform MSM Unify; pathways, PPPs, and TNE; and a full suite of solutions serving HEIs, recruitment agents, and the 360-degree international student journey.

Sanjay specialises in strategic partnerships, human capital management, and marketing and communications for astounding results in business growth and development, market entry and expansion, and a first-to-market advantage. As a dynamic investor in technology, education, and real estate, he is actively involved in nurturing promising ventures that align with his values and vision, whether seedinginnovation in edtech or capitalising on prime real estate, and drive societal advancement.

Tejal Laul, Director

Tejal is a Director for Laul Global Ventures, managing real estate investments in Canada, particularly in British Columbia. She is also the COO of Innivec, an education group managing private post secondary institutions across Canada. Innivec boasts 4 brands and 11 campuses in Canada, namely Multihexa College, Eton College Canada, Taylor Pro College, and Extreme Pro Training.

From skill training projects and government collaborations to licensing or franchising campuses, Tejal is actively seeking partnerships and joint ventures worldwide. She is also open to connecting with realtors and property owners with viable opportunities in Vancouver, Kelowna, and nearby areas.
